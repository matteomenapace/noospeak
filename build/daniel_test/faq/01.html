  1. What is NooSpeak?
    NooSpeak is a revolutionary approach to Web search. Instead of squeezing your brain to find a bunch of keywords, you can speak to Google the way you normally speak (what scientists call “natural language”). If you are familiar with online chats (what scientists call “instant messaging software”) you know what we mean.

  2. That's pretty cool. How does it do that?
    Google's database has become pretty vast in the last few years, thanks to millions of people using Google’s services everyday. That’s Google’s memory. NooSpeak brain uses a wide range of algorithms to predict the answers users are most likely to want to see.

  3. Google works well as is. Why should I use NooSpeak?
    By using a language you are naturally familiar with, NooSpeak can make your online experience more convenient and efficient. NooSpeak might answer in ways that you will find novel or intriguing. We think that NooSpeak is just much easier and funnier than the good old Google Search.

  4. How do I get back to normal Google?
    You can go directly to Google, or type http://www.google.com into your browser's address field; either way, you'll be back using ordinary Google search without NooSpeak.

  5. How about a few examples of when it works best?
    NooSpeak can handle an average conversation quite well. You can follow the most popular topics, suggested in real time, or you can try and engage Google in a conversation about Wittgenstein’s games of language, if you dare.

  6. When will you support other languages?
    We're not sure yet. But we're always interested in expanding our products into more languages, and hope to be able to offer you such services soon! Anyway, if you select the unfiltered mode (see answer X to learn more) you will be able to chat in languages other than English.

  7. Here's my comment about NooSpeak. Ready to write this down?
    We love feedback. Please send your NooSpeak comments, criticisms and suggestions to us at i.am.dissatisfied@gmail.com - We always read every email our users send us.

  8. I don't want anyone to know I'm into lemur racing. Is my information private?
    Yes, your information is private. NooSpeak does not base its conversation on your personal searches, although it does use information about the relative popularity of common topics to generate its sentences.

  9. Can NooSpeak see what I'm typing?
    Just as E.T.(TM) needs to phone home in order to get a spaceship to pick him up, NooSpeak needs to talk to Google’s database while you type in order to keep the conversation going. Everything you type, though, is protected by Google's privacy policy.

  10. How do filtering settings affect my conversations with NooSpeak?
    NooSpeak uses Artificial Intelligence to filter Web pages in order to provide you with sentences that make sense in a human way.
    You can choose from among three Filtering settings:
    • Strict filtering No Web content is used to chat with you in this case, but just Artificial Intelligence. Responses make always sense, but they become repetitive after a while.
    • Moderate filtering Artificial Intelligence is enhanced with Web content. This is your default setting; you’ll receive moderate filtering unless you change it.
    And finally…
    • No Filtering As you’ve probably figured out, turns off Artificial Intelligence filtering completely.
